About Us

Founded in June 2016, PowerComm Solutions has swiftly become a leader in supplying top-notch power, telecoms, and electrical accessories. Our journey began with a vision to transform the industry with exceptional service and high-quality products. As a 100% black-owned company, we are dedicated to diversity and excellence in all we do.

Our team of experienced professionals is committed to delivering tailored solutions that meet the unique needs of our clients. We pride ourselves on our strong relationships with customers, suppliers, and manufacturers, which form the foundation of our success.

With over 20 years of collective industry experience, we have grown rapidly and established ourselves as a key supplier to municipalities and major organizations, including Eskom and the City of Cape Town.
